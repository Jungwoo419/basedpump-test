How It Works

Based Pump utilized the mint.club SDK to offer a fair launch with no pre-sale and no team allocation for token minting. The 100 million tokens from the creator’s free minting are reserved for liquidity deposits when the token’s market cap reaches $69k, which happens automatically.

Step 1 Pick a coin that you like, or launch a coin yourself
Step 2 Buy the coin using the bonding curve.
Step 3 Sell at any time to lock in your profits or losses.
Step 4 Once enough people buy on the bonding curve, the market cap reaches $69k.
Step 5 $12k of liquidity is then deposited into Uniswap V3 and burned.
